Our Campaign Priorities

Our path extends from the true north of our strong liberal arts tradition - rigorous inquiry, generous mentorship and multiple viewpoints. Our destination is clear: To equip citizens and leaders with the knowledge and skills, courage and confidence to propel society forward.
