The West Quad

The West Quad enables us to make major strides toward a more accessible Kenyon campus. We estimate that when all of the West Quad projects are complete 90% of classrooms will be accessible, up from 71 percent currently; 100 percent of classrooms with 20 - 99-seat capacity will be accessible.
